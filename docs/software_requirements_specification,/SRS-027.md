**Asset Register & Common Property Accounting**

**System:** GharMitra ‚Äì Housing Society ERP
**Derived from:** CR-2026-GM-FIN-013
**Purpose:** To record, manage, depreciate and audit all common-area assets of a housing society.

---

## 1. Purpose

This SRS defines how GharMitra shall maintain a **legally valid Asset Register** for all common-area assets owned by a housing society, including assets handed over by the builder and assets purchased later by the society.

This ensures:

* Correct balance sheet
* Audit compliance
* Insurance support
* Ownership clarity

---

## 2. Scope

This module shall:

* Maintain asset master data
* Track asset value and depreciation
* Link assets to accounting vouchers
* Generate statutory asset reports

This module shall NOT:

* Store personal documents
* Handle inventory or consumables

---

## 3. Asset Categories

The system shall support, at minimum:

* Lift & elevator systems
* DG sets & electrical systems
* Water pumps, sumps, tanks
* Fire safety systems
* Gym & recreation equipment
* Furniture & fixtures
* Clubhouse & community hall assets
* Any other common-area property

## 5. Asset Creation Rules

### FR-A1

Assets handed over by the builder shall be entered through **Opening Balance** using a **Journal Voucher**.

Accounting entry:

```
Asset Accounts  Dr
   To Corpus Fund
```

### FR-A2

Assets purchased later shall be created via **Payment Voucher** and automatically added to Asset Register.

Accounting entry:

```
Asset Account Dr
   To Bank / Cash
```

---

## 6. Depreciation

### FR-A3

System shall calculate depreciation annually or monthly.

Accounting entry:

```
Depreciation Expense  Dr
   To Accumulated Depreciation
```

### FR-A4

Depreciation shall reduce asset book value but not its historical cost.

---

## 7. Asset Disposal

### FR-A5

When an asset is sold or scrapped, system shall:

* Remove it from active assets
* Post gain/loss via Journal Voucher
* Keep history for audit
