## 1. PURPOSE

This SRS defines the requirements for implementing a **Source-Document-based accounting system** in GharMitra using:

* Receipt Vouchers
* Payment Vouchers
* Transfer Vouchers
* Journal Vouchers
* Maintenance Invoices

The objective is to achieve **legally compliant, audit-ready, and user-friendly RWA accounting**.

---

## 2. SCOPE

This SRS covers:

* Financial data entry
* Bill generation
* Voucher posting
* Member & vendor accounting
* Cash & bank tracking
* Registers & audit controls
* PDF generation

This system must support Indian **Apartment Owners Associations / RWAs**.

| Term                      | Meaning                                 |
| ------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Receipt Voucher (RV)      | Document recording money received       |
| Payment Voucher (PV)      | Document recording money paid           |
| Transfer Voucher (TV)     | Movement between society‚Äôs own accounts |
| Journal Voucher (JV)      | Non-cash accounting entry               |

## 4. SYSTEM OVERVIEW

GharMitra must be driven by **Source Documents** instead of free-form entries.

All financial data must originate from one of:

* Receipt
* Payment
* Transfer
* Journal
* Invoice

No ledger entry shall exist without a voucher.

---

## 5. FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS

### 5.1 Receipt Voucher (RV)

#### FR-RV-1

System shall allow creating Receipt Vouchers.

#### FR-RV-2

Receipt Voucher shall capture:

* Receipt Number (auto)
* Date
* Received Into (Bank / Cash)
* Received From (Flat / Person / Bank)
* Reference (Cheque / UTR)
* Description

#### FR-RV-3

Receipt shall support **multiple allocations**:
| Account | Amount |

#### FR-RV-4

System shall automatically post:

```
Bank / Cash   Dr
    Allocated Accounts (Income / Member)
```

#### FR-RV-5

System shall generate **Receipt PDF**.

---

### 5.2 Payment Voucher (PV)

#### FR-PV-1

System shall allow creating Payment Vouchers.

#### FR-PV-2

Payment Voucher shall capture:

* Voucher Number
* Date
* Paid From (Bank / Cash)
* Paid To (Vendor)
* Reference
* Description

#### FR-PV-3

Payment shall support **multiple expense allocations**.

#### FR-PV-4

System shall automatically post:

```
Expense Accounts   Dr
    Bank / Cash
```

#### FR-PV-5

System shall generate **Payment Voucher PDF**.

---

### 5.3 Transfer Voucher (TV)

#### FR-TV-1

System shall support transfers between society‚Äôs own accounts.

#### FR-TV-2

Fields:

* From Account
* To Account
* Amount
* Date

#### FR-TV-3

System shall post:

```
To Account   Dr
    From Account
```

---

### 5.4 Journal Voucher (JV)

#### FR-JV-1

Journal Voucher shall be used only for **non-cash entries**.

#### FR-JV-2

System shall prevent Bank or Cash accounts in JV.

#### FR-JV-3

JV shall support multi-line debit and credit.

#### FR-JV-4

Total Debit must equal Total Credit.

---

### 5.5 Maintenance Invoice

#### FR-INV-1

System shall generate monthly maintenance bills.

#### FR-INV-2

Invoice creation shall auto-post:

```
Flat Account   Dr
    Maintenance Income
```

#### FR-INV-3

Invoice PDF shall include:

* Society details
* Flat details
* Month
* Due date
* Line items
* Amount in words
* Overdue indicator
